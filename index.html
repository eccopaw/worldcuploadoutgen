Ruleset

• 4 slots per player. Farm is mandatory and occupies the first slot.
• Pool 1 (−1): Shotgunner, Plasma Trooper, DJ, Commander, Mortar, Marksman, Fragger — each removes one slot from your teammate.
• Pool 2 (±0): Cryo-Gunner, Flamethrower, Mercenary, Graveyard, Commando, Tuber, Patrol, Railgunner, Enforcer, Golden Commando — no effect on teammate.
• Pool 3 (+1): Barracks, Tweeter, Phaser, Sniper, Zed — each grants one slot to your teammate.
• Both players may run the same tower. A pairing is valid when neither player's picks exceed their adjusted slot count.
